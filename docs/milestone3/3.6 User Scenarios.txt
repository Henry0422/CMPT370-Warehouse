3.6 - User Scenarios 
For Shipper
- Shipper may want to know the upcoming shipments, and assign upcoming shipments to a Stock Picker.
- Shipper may want to know the future shipments plan to make schedule.
- Shipper may want to record to outgoing shipment information.

For Receiver
- Receiver may want to record the information of incoming products.
- Receiver may want to check if there is capacity in the warehouse for a given purchase order.
- Receiver may want to check which shipments will need to be shipped before the warehouse has enough capacity
